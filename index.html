Halloween Masquerade

Our group costumes

The Fifth Element

"This is probably one of my favorite group costumes because we all got really into creating these characters. Some of us put a lot more work into it than others, but they all turned out great."

Plants vs Zombies ™

"My friend who is the genius with paper mache helped me a lot on this one. It takes a lot of time to create these heads, but the end result is great. The one thing that I didn't like about this costume was how hard it was to hear or be heard. I ended taking the head off a lot throughout the night just so I could have conversations. "

Nickelodeon

"We had a lot more people join in for this group costume because we all grew up watching Nickelodeon and had our favorite characters. I steered clear of the paper mache this time because I learned my lesson with the previous year. I really thought mine turned out great but people still didn't know who I was. I had Cynthia, I thought that was a dead give away. Guess not."

Marvel

"This was by far the largest turn out we had for the group costumes. I didn't realize so many people were into comics, although I should have known with all of the movies that are centered around them. I don't know them too well though so I had help coming up with who I was going as. I'm the Scarlet Witch, in case others are also unfamiliar with Marvel."

Pixel

"This was the group costumes theme for 2017. However I did a bad job of documenting this past year and didn't get a group photo. My friend's recently adopted a dog that looks a lot like the dog from Up. So they went with that movie. As soon as I heard that I thought squirrel! So I created a squirrel. It took a long time, but turned out great."
